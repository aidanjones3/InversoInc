Design · Develop · Build

INVERSO, Inc., is a design- build development company specializing in residential construction with over 15 years experience. We have designed and built projects in Chicago, Illinois, Boca Raton, Florida and Big Sky, Montana.

Our design-build approach sets us apart from the rest....the design-build process saves time and money for you, the client. We bring value engineering into the design process, at the onset of a project. Deisgn-build brings members of a project together early in the planning process to identify and address issues of cost, schedule and constructability. This dynamic process allows for creativity, efficiency, and overall cohesion.

Building with us.... Our clients choose us because we create a fun experience, we deliver an amazing product, and we exceed their expectations. We are partners in success with the engineers, trades, and suppliers on your project. We are fair, communicative, respectful, fun and do all we can to make sure you are happy.

About Us

Joe Capozzoli, Principal:

Joe has fifteen plus years in the commercial/residential development and construction business started in Chicago. In addition to creating custom built homes and furniture Joe is also a licensed attorney in the state of Montana.


Michele Capozzoli, Managing Partner:

Michele has 10 years in the commercial and construction business started in Chicago after a career in hotel/restaurant management. Michele's focus is guiding clients through interior design decisions from setting a budget, directing attention to high quality products/styles, creating a cohesive design that you want to live in all while having fun!
